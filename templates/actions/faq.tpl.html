General Questions

How do I get a quote?

You can always get a quote using the Order Now page.

Once you fill in the number of pages, the total cost will be shown automatically above the "Order Now" button. You can also choose the necessary options and the total will be adjusted automatically.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

We have a discount policy for bulk orders. The extent of discount depends on the scope and challenge in work.

Discounts also depend on the potential of long term partnership. Please feel free to contact us.

What is your turnaround time?

We have two turnaround packages: 3 days turnaround and 1 day turnaround, you can calculate the turnaround time on our order page by select the turnaround package and the number of pages.

What are your working hours?

We have two turnaround packages: 3 days turnaround and 1 day turnaround, you can calculate the turnaround time on our order page by select the turnaround package and the number of pages.

  • AEST: 10:00am-7:00pm
  • GMT: 1:00am-10:00am
  • EST: 8:00pm-5:00am

Please be aware that being we don't work weekends, those days are taking into consideration for our scheduled deadlines. If you require time-line adjustments, we can investigate the possibility and possible weekend work.

What if I need some changes in markup provided by you?

Our aim is to provide you with 100% service satisfaction. If you ever wish to make changes to your design, even though our programmers have already started the implementation or your order is complete, just contact us. We feel that for every problem, there is a happy solution that satisfies everyone. You can always talk to us about discounts too.

How can I get in touch with you?

We will provide you access to our Client Area, which features updates about the project status, so you will be aware of your project at any time.

You may also contact us using Online Chat and speak directly with your project manager or get consultation from our Support Team.
