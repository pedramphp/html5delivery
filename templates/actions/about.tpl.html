How Our Process Works

  • 1

    Step One

    It all starts with you. You place the order, and our amazing "Customer Happiness Agents" will be contacting you right away. We take a look at your order and make sure everything is exactly the way you want it.

  • 2

    Step Two

    After your project specs have been checked out and payment has been received, we are ready to get started on your project. This is when your project is born and is assigned to one of our HTML/CSS guys.

  • 3

    Step Three

    After these HTML/CSS gurus are done slicin' and dicing' your designs and writing your code, they go ahead and throughly cross-browser test your entire site. We have a whole bunch of slave virtual machines set up here at the office to test on every browser imaginable! Seriously!

  • 4

    Step Four

    After these wonderful programmers finish, they send it over to our QA obsessive engineers. These guys are insanely detail oriented (You know the kind of guys who cut lawns with scissors?). They check your programmer's work and your designs to make sure it is exactly what you wanted. Voila!

  • 5

    Step Five

    Your project manager will go ahead and post your HTML/CSS project.

  • 6

    Step Six

    Ahh, Implementations. After we get that beautiful HTML/CSS approved by you, we will send it over to the required implementations department, depending on which CMS you chose. Have no fear, as we have teams that specialize in the exact implementation you choose.

  • 7

    Step Seven

    Success. Your order is finished and of course you leave 110% happy, because like always, we exceeded your expectations. :)
